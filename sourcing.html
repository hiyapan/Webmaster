Our Sourcing Philosophy



Local First


We partner with nearby farms and suppliers to bring you the freshest produce, reducing our carbon footprint while supporting local growers.

Seasonal Selections


Our menu changes with the seasons to celebrate the best nature has to offer. This ensures every ingredient is at its peak of flavor, quality, and sustainability.

Organic and Non-GMO

Whenever possible, we choose organize and non-GMO produce, ensuring your meals are free from harmful chemicals and as pure as nature intended.

Fair Trade Commitment

For ingredients like spices and exotic fruits, we prioritize fair trade partnerships. This ensures that farmers and workers aroudn the world are paid fair wages and work in ethical conditions. By choosing fair trade products, we support sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and empower communities.

Reducing Food Waste

We take pride in our commitment to waste reduction. By working closely with suppliers, we minimize over-ordering and focus on using every part of the ingredients we source. Leftover ingredients inspire menu creations or are composted to enrich the soil of local farms.
