Appetizer

Rustic Roots Hummus Plate

Creamy hummus made with locally sourced chickpeas, roasted heirloom carrots, and a drizzle of herb-infused olive oil. Served with freshly baked sourdough and crispy veggie chips.

Harvest Croquettes

Lightly fried vegetable croquettes made with "ugly" root veggies, paired with a tangy cashew crema and microgreens.

Global Garden Rolls

Rice paper rolls packed with seasonal vegetables, herbs, and glass noodles. Served with tamarind-peanut dipping sauce.

Seasonal Soup Shot Trio

A flight of three mini soups featuring seasonal flavors, such as roasted tomato-basil, curried butternut squash, and spiced lentil.

Entrees

Mosaic Earth Bowl

A vibrant grain bowl featuring quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, marinated tempeh, pickled red cabbage, and a creamy avocado-lime dressing.

Spiced Cauliflower Tagine

Slow-cooked cauliflower in a Moroccan-inspired sauce with apricots, almonds, and warming spices. Served with fluffy herbed couscous.

Global Street Tacos

Three tacos inspired by global flavors: roasted jackfruit carnitas, coconut curry chickpeas, and smoky mushroom asada. Topped with house-made slaw and fresh salsa.

Stuffed Eggplant Moussaka

Baked eggplant layered with lentil-walnut filling, creamy cashew béchamel, and fresh herbs. Served with a side of citrus-dressed greens.

Dessert

Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Rich and velvety dark chocolate mousse made with ripe avocados, sweetened with maple syrup. Topped with cacao nibs and seasonal berries.

Carrot Cake Parfait

Layers of spiced carrot cake crumbles, vegan cream cheese frosting, and candied walnuts served in a mason jar.

Mango Sticky Rice Brûlée

A twist on the classic, featuring coconut-infused sticky rice topped with caramelized mango and a crackly brûlée topping.

Seasonal Sorbet Flight

A trio of house-made sorbets highlighting the freshest local fruit, such as blackberry-lavender, blood orange, and peach-basil.
