About Us

At Mosaic, we bring together the flavors of international cuisines, all through the lens of plant-based ingredients. From savory curries to Mediterranean-inspired dishes, every meal is crafted to celebrate the diversity of global cooking while staying true to a plant-powered approach.

Our mission is simple: to create food that’s both satisfying and sustainable. By sourcing eco-friendly, feel-good ingredients, we’re committed to offering a dining experience that nourishes the body and respects the planet. At Mosaic, we’re dedicated to serving dishes that offer great flavor and a sense of purpose.

At Mosaic, every dish is a celebration

We aim to highlight the vibrant flavors of the world, fusing tradition with innovation

Our Food

At Mosaic, our cuisine is a celebration of global flavors brought together through plant-based creativity. Drawing inspiration from the rich culinary traditions of countries around the world, we craft dishes that honor their roots while offering a fresh, modern twist. From the bold spices of South Asia to the comforting herbs of the Mediterranean, each plate tells a story of culture, connection, and sustainability.

Our menu is designed to showcase the incredible versatility of plant-based ingredients. Every dish is thoughtfully prepared to highlight natural flavors, creating meals that are as nourishing as they are delicious. Whether you’re exploring a new cuisine or rediscovering a familiar favorite, Mosaic invites you to enjoy the world on a plate.
