Our Mission

At Mosaic, we bring together the flavors of international cuisines, all through the lens of plant-based ingredients. From savory curries to Mediterranean-inspired dishes, every meal is crafted to celebrate the diversity of global cooking.

Our mission is simple: to create food that’s both satisfying and sustainable. By sourcing eco-friendly, feel-good ingredients, we’re committed to offering a dining experience that nourishes the body and respects the planet.

At Mosaic, every dish is a celebration

We aim to highlight the vibrant flavors of the world, fusing tradition with innovation

Our Food

Our cuisine is a celebration of global flavors and plant-based creativity- from the bold spices of South Asia to the comforting herbs of the Mediterranean, each plate tells a story.

Our restaurant's menu is designed to showcase the incredible versatility of plant-based ingredients. Every dish is prepared to highlight natural flavors, creating meals that are both nourishing and delicious. Mosaic invites you to enjoy the world on a plate.
